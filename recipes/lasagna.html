LASAGNA

Description

This easy lasagna recipe is a classic comfort food made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese.

Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this dish is hearty, flavorful, and even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef with garlic and onion, then add tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella and parmesan on top.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, then let rest before serving.
