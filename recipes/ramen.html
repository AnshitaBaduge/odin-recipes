RAMEN

Description

This simple ramen recipe is a quick and comforting meal made with noodles, savory broth, and flavorful toppings.

Perfect for busy days, it can be customized with vegetables, eggs, or meat for extra flavor and nutrition.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Green onions
  • Boiled egg
  • Vegetables (mushrooms, carrots, or spinach)

Steps

  1. Bring the broth to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add garlic and soy sauce to the broth.
  3. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender.

  5. Serve hot and top with a boiled egg and green onions.
