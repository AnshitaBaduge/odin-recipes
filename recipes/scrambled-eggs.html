SCRAMBLED-EGGS

Description

Scrambled eggs are a quick and easy breakfast classic made with just a few simple ingredients.

Soft, fluffy, and delicious, they are perfect on their own or served with toast and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk or cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and let them sit briefly.
  4. Gently stir until soft curds form.
  5. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
